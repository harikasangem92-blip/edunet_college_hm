History

SRITW College was founded with the goal of providing top-quality technical education exclusively for women. Over the years, it has grown into a renowned institution with state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty.

Campus & Facilities

  • Modern classrooms and laboratories
  • Library with extensive resources
  • Sports and extracurricular facilities
  • Wi-Fi enabled campus
  • Hostel and cafeteria facilities

Achievements

SRITW students have excelled in academic, technical, and cultural events nationally and internationally. Our alumni hold key positions in industry and research organizations.
